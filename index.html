The Problem

You have the experience.
The ATS just can't read it.

01

Most applicants have solid experience — internships, projects, clubs — but write about it in the wrong language for the role they're targeting.

02

ATS systems scan for specific keywords from job descriptions. If your resume doesn't match, it never reaches a recruiter — regardless of your actual qualifications.

03

The gap isn't ability. It's translation. You need to reframe what you've done in the language of what companies are hiring for.

The Solution

Reinterpret your experience
from 3 angles.

GOalign structures your experience through the PDCA framework, then reinterprets it from three perspectives — job function, industry, and role — to surface the precise keywords recruiters and ATS systems are scanning for.
